Role-Based Recommendations
Solo SDR / Freelancer
Dux-Soup
Lowest entry price ($11.25/mo), LinkedIn-native drip campaigns, safety limits to stay compliant. Perfect for individuals who need cost-effective LinkedIn automation without complexity.
Runner-up: PhantomBuster (Start plan $56/mo — more platforms)
Tech-Savvy Growth Team
PhantomBuster
100+ pre-built automations, 15+ platforms, unlimited users on all plans. Ideal for scraping, enrichment, and multi-step workflows across LinkedIn, Sales Nav, and beyond.
Runner-up: Meet Alfred (simpler multi-channel sequences)
Small Team / Multi-Channel
Meet Alfred
$29/user/mo with LinkedIn + Email + Twitter sequences, Smart Inbox, AI personalization, and white-label options. Best all-in-one automation for small outreach teams.
Runner-up: PhantomBuster (wider platform reach)
Enterprise Sales Rep
LinkedIn Sales Navigator
Native LinkedIn integration, 50+ filters, Buyer Intent data, Relationship Map, and deep CRM sync with Salesforce/HubSpot. The gold standard for enterprise LinkedIn prospecting.
Pair with: Cognism for verified phone numbers
Startup / Modern CRM
Attio
Free up to 3 seats, AI agent (Ask Attio), ICP fit scoring, MCP server, GDPR/ISO certified. The most flexible modern CRM for startups that want AI built-in from day one.
Runner-up: Meet Alfred (more outreach channels)
Agency / No-Touch Outreach
Cleverly
Fully managed campaigns with a dedicated account manager, inbox management, and A/B testing. Pay for results, not tools — ideal if you want lead gen done for you.
Note: starts at $397/mo — no free trial
Data-Heavy SDR Team
Cognism
Verified mobile numbers, GDPR/CCPA compliance across 15+ countries, Bombora intent signals, and Diamond Data® for hard-to-find contacts. Best pure data provider in the set.
Pair with: Sales Navigator or Meet Alfred for outreach
